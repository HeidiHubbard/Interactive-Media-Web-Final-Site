Hummingbird Spirits is a small, afro-Indigenous owned business that believe that access to traditional, spiritual medicines are an important part of maintaining a healthy mind, body and spirit. Customary use of medicines are a common practice among many nations across the world, therefore providing safe, affordable accessiblity to sustainably and ethically sourced products is their top priority. Their place within the wellness community is to share the responsibility of helping others. They absolutely support nation to nation knowledge shares on usage of customary medicines and spiritual products and practices. Gatekeeping is absolutely not a part of Hummingbird Spirits’ operations.
