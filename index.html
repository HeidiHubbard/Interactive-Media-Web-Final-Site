I'm Heidi, but friends call me hub!

I am a passionate, purpouse-driven Indigenous creative who is determined to reshape the design and advertsing worlds to be more inclusive and equitable towards our communities most in need.

more about me

As an Indigenous creative, I recognize and value the importance of responsibility, equity and integrity in my design practice. The design, and advertising industry in particular, has been long dominated by communications that do more harm than good. I am determined to change this by prioriziting like-minded small businesses and brands, offering a safe space to co-create creative solutions for all design needs — because there are so many barriers to success in developing, growing, and sustaining a small business, and respectful, equitable, quality design shouldn’t be one of them!
