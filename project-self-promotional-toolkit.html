This is a self promotional toolkit “business card” leave behind for my brand, that encappsulates a personal part of myself to share with possible connections to remember me by. The concept relates to my Indigenous Mi’maq heritage. Growing up in rural south-west Nova Scotia, my family taught me values of respecting and connecting with nature. I’ve carried these values with me throughout my life, even as I’ve foundmyself in new environments like urban Toronto. Merging my love for nature and design, I’ve specially curated a seed bundle for creatives.
